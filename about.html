Who is

Naomie Kosgei?

Ask Naomie Kosgei what she does, and the answer might surprise you. SHe won’t say She’s a songwriter even though he’s written more than 20 songs. SHe won’t say she’s a worship leader even though he’s led worship in Many churches in kenya. If you ask Naomie why God put him on this planet, he’ll tell you, “To be an architect who designs products and events that help people experience God’s presence in a new and fresh way.” And in more 5 years of ministry, she’s had lots of opportunity to fulfill that purpose. Her discography reflects her passion to create resources for the church that lead people into an honest and intimate relationship with the Lord.
